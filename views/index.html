Head-Bobble Airdrumist

Wha?

This is a simple webapp. It plays around with Html5 motion capture and audio apis to create a cool virtual sort-of drum-kit. We worked on it all night and think it's pretty cool. You should play around with it and let us know what you think.

Why?

This app was made over a sleepless twenty four hours as a part of the Codemkrs Music Hackathon 2013 . This was not our initial app. Our initial app is less showy and more useful - an application for tourists and residents to explore the deep musical history of New Orleans. This was the idea of music author Dave Walkder and we loved it. After considering our requirements and before booting up our nodes and rubies Neel Sus insisted we try an app-maker. We did, and after a few hours on ShoutEm our app was basically done Chagrin! What to do? Something fun and hacky of course. We did this.
